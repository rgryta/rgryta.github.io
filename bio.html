Studies

In 2015 joined Warsaw University of Technology on Bachelor programme with Automatic Control and Robotics major. Over the years learned OOP languages as well as more low-level oriented technologies like PLC, SCADA and microcontroller programming.
As a thesis project worked on an analysis of 6-DOF manipulator structure by simulating various tasks with different wrist build configurations. Project was received positively receiving "very good" reviews with final grade "above good" in 2020.

Immediately after getting BScEng degree followed the studies by joining Master programme with the same major. Continuing studying different predictive regulator algorithms and system modelling as well as designing more advanced, scattered and composite systems.
For masters' project, system for smartphone owner authentication authentication was created in order to analyse different types of identification and anomaly detection using behavioral data during handheld usage. Such technology could be used as a second security layer on top of typical biometrics and/or PIN layers.
The thesis was received with "very good" grade following by the same final mark, completing Master of Science in Engineering degree.

Currently working on writing an article based on master thesis research and pursuing MBA degree.
